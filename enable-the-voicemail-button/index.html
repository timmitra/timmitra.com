Enable the Voicemail button

November 20, 2007 timmitra Leave a comment

Google your voicemail retrieval number. Mine is Rogers in Toronto 416-357-3229.

Then open the Phone.app (Green button) and click the keypad.

Enter *5005*86*yourretrievalnumber#
Press Call
then your Voicemail Button will work.
